How It Works

When enabled, sections are reordered per-subscriber based on engagement history. Subscribers who consistently interact with certain sections will see those sections higher in their newsletter. This increases relevance and drives better open and click-through rates.

The system uses subscriber interest profiles (engagement scores per section) to sort sections at assembly time. Subscribers without engagement data receive the default section order.
