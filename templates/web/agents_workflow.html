The Autonomous Newsroom

TrueFans NEWSLETTERS runs on a team of AI agents, each with a specialized role. Together they research, write, edit, review, and promote 3 newsletter editions (Fan, Artist, Industry) across 3 send days per week — producing 9 complete issues every week with minimal human oversight.

The Production Cycle — 5 Steps

1
Research
Researcher fetches content from 120+ RSS feeds and web sources. Scores relevance. Tags by section.
2
Plan
Editor-in-Chief selects sections, assigns writers, creates editorial calendar.
3
Write
29 specialist writers generate drafts using section-specific prompts and research data.
4
Review
Editor reviews each draft. Human checkpoint for approval, rewrite requests, or tone adjustments.
5
Publish
Assemble approved drafts, inject sponsors, generate subject lines, schedule send.

Autonomy Levels

Supervised Agents generate content but all output requires human approval before publishing Review every draft, approve/reject/rewrite
Semi-Autonomous Agents can auto-approve content that scores above quality thresholds Spot-check flagged items, handle exceptions
Autonomous Full auto-pilot — research, write, review, publish without human intervention Monitor dashboards, adjust strategy
