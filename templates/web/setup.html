DNS Setup Guide

Configure these DNS records to maximize email deliverability and avoid spam filters.

SPF Record

Add a TXT record to your domain's DNS:

v=spf1 include:mailgun.org ~all

Replace mailgun.org with your email provider's SPF include if different.

DKIM Record

Add the DKIM TXT record from your email provider. This is typically a long TXT record at a subdomain like:

smtp._domainkey.yourdomain.com  TXT  "v=DKIM1; k=rsa; p=YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY"

Your email provider (Mailgun, SES, Postmark, etc.) will give you the exact record to add.

DMARC Record

Add a TXT record at _dmarc.yourdomain.com:

v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourdomain.com

Start with p=none to monitor, then move to p=quarantine or p=reject once verified.

Tracking Subdomain

Create a CNAME record for click/open tracking:

trk.yourdomain.com  CNAME  your-email-provider-tracking-domain

Using a custom tracking domain improves deliverability and link reputation.
