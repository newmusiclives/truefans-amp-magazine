Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the license?

You get full access to the TrueFans platform for your city: AI content generation across 29 writer personas, 3 newsletter editions (Fan, Artist, Industry), subscriber management tools, sponsor slot management, analytics dashboard, email delivery, and ongoing platform updates. You also get a white-label portal with your branding.

How does revenue sharing work?

You keep 80% of all revenue generated from your city edition — including subscriber revenue, sponsor payments, affiliate commissions, and paid tier upgrades. The 20% platform share covers infrastructure, AI content generation, email delivery, and ongoing development.

Can I choose any city?

We currently have 20+ city markets available, with more launching regularly. If your city isn't listed, apply anyway — we evaluate new markets based on demand and local music scene activity. Each city is exclusive to one licensee.

Do I need technical skills?

No. The platform handles all the technical work: email delivery, subscriber management, analytics, and content generation. Your job is to review AI-generated content, build local relationships with sponsors, and grow your subscriber base.

What if I want to cancel?

Monthly licenses can be cancelled anytime. Annual licenses are non-refundable but can be cancelled at renewal. Your subscriber list remains yours. We offer a 30-day free trial so you can evaluate the platform before committing.
